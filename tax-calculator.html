About Tax Calculations

Tax calculations help you estimate your total tax liability or potential refund for the year based on your income, deductions, filing status, and taxes already withheld. Understanding your tax situation can help with financial planning and avoiding surprises at tax time.

The **Tax Calculator** provides an estimate of your federal and state tax obligations based on common tax brackets and standard deduction amounts. This tool is designed to give you a general understanding of your tax situation, but actual tax liability may vary based on specific circumstances, credits, and additional deductions.

2

3
