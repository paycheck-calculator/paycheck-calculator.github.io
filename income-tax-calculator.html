About Income Tax Calculations

Income tax is a tax imposed on individuals or entities that varies with their respective income or profits. The calculator uses a progressive tax system, meaning higher incomes are taxed at higher marginal rates. This tool provides an estimate based on simplified tax brackets and common deduction amounts.

The Income Tax Calculator specifically estimates your Federal Income Tax using the 2024 tax brackets. It calculates your Adjusted Gross Income (AGI), determines your Taxable Income after deductions, and then applies the progressive tax rates based on your selected filing status. It also includes an estimation for state tax based on the simple percentage you provide.

Note: This calculation does not include other federal taxes like Social Security or Medicare, nor does it factor in tax credits, which can significantly reduce your final tax liability. This tool is for estimation purposes only.

How the Income Tax Calculator Works

1

Determine Taxable Income

Your Gross Income minus Deductions equals your estimated Taxable Income (this is what is subject to federal income tax).

2

Apply Federal Tax Brackets

The Taxable Income is applied to the progressive 2024 Federal Income Tax Brackets for your chosen Filing Status to find your Federal Tax Liability.

3

Calculate Refund/Owed

The total estimated tax (Federal + State) is compared to your pre-paid Withholdings to determine your estimated Refund (if positive) or Amount Owed (if negative).
