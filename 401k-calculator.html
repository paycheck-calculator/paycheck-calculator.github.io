About 401K Calculations

A 401(k) calculator uses the principle of compound interest to project how much your retirement savings will grow over time, accounting for both your personal contributions and your employer's matching contributions.

The 401K Calculator estimates the future value of your account by applying an assumed annual return rate to your current balance and the sum of all future annual contributions (including match). This projection is a powerful tool for retirement planning, showing the long-term benefit of consistent saving and employer matching.

How the 401K Calculator Works

1

Input Balances & Salary

Enter your starting 401K balance and your current gross annual salary.

2

Set Contribution Parameters

Input your personal contribution rate (%), employer match rate (%), expected annual return (%), and years until retirement.

3

Project Future Value

The calculator displays the estimated future value, total contributions, total employer match, and total investment earnings.
