Privacy Policy for Paycheck Calculator

Last updated: December 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Paycheck Calculator (the "Site," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you visit our website at https://paycheck-calculator.github.io/ (the "Website").

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Policy applies solely to information collected through the Website.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

The Website is a free online paycheck and salary calculator tool. It operates entirely in your browser using JavaScript. When you use the calculator, you may input personal financial information such as gross pay, tax filing status, pay frequency, and other details to estimate net pay and deductions.

  • We do not collect, store, or process any of this information on our servers.
  • All calculations are performed locally on your device.
  • No data you enter is transmitted to us or any third party.

Automatically Collected Information

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics tools (such as Google Analytics), or any other tracking technologies on the Website.

The Website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub, as the hosting provider, may automatically collect certain technical information from visitors, such as IP addresses and access logs, for security, maintenance, and legal compliance purposes.

For details on how GitHub handles this information, please review the GitHub Privacy Statement.

We have no access to or control over this data collected by GitHub.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information from users of the Website, there is no use, sharing, or disclosure of such information by us.

Third-Party Links

The Website may contain links to external sites (e.g., tax authority websites or related resources). These sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

Children's Privacy

The Website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
