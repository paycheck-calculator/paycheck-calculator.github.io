Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

1. Introduction

The Paycheck Calculator ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at https://paycheck-calculator.github.io/. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Data Collection and Use

Personal Data

We do not collect any personal identifying information (PII) from users for the operation of the calculator tools. All financial calculations are performed locally in your web browser and are not transmitted to our servers.

Usage Data (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to monitor and analyze the use of our Service. Google Analytics collects information such as your computer's Internet Protocol address (IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages. This data is anonymized and used to improve the performance and usability of the website.

3. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
