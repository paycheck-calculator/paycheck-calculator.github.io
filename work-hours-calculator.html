About Work Hours Calculation

A Work Hours Calculator helps you determine the total time spent working, which is essential for accurate timesheets, payroll, and project management. It simplifies the process of calculating shift duration while automatically accounting for breaks.

The Work Hours Calculator takes your shift's start time, end time, and break duration to give you a net total of working hours. This tool is designed for quick estimation, providing the result in either decimal format (e.g., 7.5 hours) for easy payroll entry or standard H:MM format (e.g., 7:30 hours).

How the Work Hours Calculator Works

1

Enter Start & End Time

Input the time your shift started and the time it ended. You can account for overnight shifts.

2

Input Break Time & Format

Enter the total time (in minutes) taken for breaks. Select your preferred output format (Decimal or H:MM).

3

View Total Hours Worked

Get the precise calculation of total hours worked after subtracting the break time.
