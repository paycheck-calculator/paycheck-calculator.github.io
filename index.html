How the Paycheck Calculator Works

1

Input Gross Pay

Enter your gross annual salary or hourly wage into the field. This is your total pay before any deductions.

2

Select Frequency & Status

Choose your pay frequency (e.g., Bi-Weekly) and your tax filing status (e.g., Single, Married).

3

Get Your Net Pay

The calculator applies estimated federal and state taxes, plus FICA (Social Security and Medicare), and displays your net take-home pay.

About the Paycheck Calculator

The Paycheck Calculator is a free, fast, and easy-to-use tool designed to help you estimate your take-home pay. Understanding your deductions for taxes, FICA, and pre-tax benefits can be complicated, but our tool provides a clear, per-pay-period breakdown.

Disclaimer: This tool uses simplified, mock tax rates for demonstration purposes. It is an estimation and not a substitute for professional payroll or tax advice. Actual tax withholdings may vary based on your W-4 elections, location, and specific circumstances.
